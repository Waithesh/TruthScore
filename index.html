    How to Check if a YouTube Video is a Scam

    The "make money online" industry on YouTube is worth billions, but it is plagued by fake gurus selling dream lifestyles that don't exist. In 2021, YouTube removed the public dislike count, making it harder than ever to spot these scams.

    Common Red Flags

    • Unrealistic Promises: Making $10,000 in 24 hours with "no work."
    • Urgency Tactics: "Only 10 spots left" or "This video is coming down."
    • Bot Comments: Repeated generic praise to bury real warnings.
