    How to Check if a YouTube Video is a Scam

    YouTube removed the dislike button in 2021, making it nearly impossible to spot scam videos at first glance. TruthScore is a free YouTube scam detector that reveals what creators hide from you.

    What Does TruthScore Check?

    • Hidden dislike ratios — via the ReturnYouTubeDislike API
    • Comment sentiment analysis — AI scans for buried complaints and scam warnings
    • Channel credibility — age, upload consistency, and historical patterns
    • Engagement anomalies — fake views, bot comments, purchased engagement
    • Manipulative language — urgency tactics, fake scarcity, psychological pressure

    Common Red Flags We Detect

    Title & Thumbnail: Unrealistic income claims, excessive emojis, fake urgency language

    Engagement Patterns: High hidden dislike ratio, disabled comments, suspiciously low engagement

    Channel History: Brand-new channel with viral video, frequent branding changes

    Types of Scams We Detect

    • Passive income scams — promise "easy money" then sell expensive courses
    • Dropshipping course scams — outdated tactics wrapped in professional production
    • Crypto / Forex scams — trading signals, automated bots, "guaranteed" strategies
    • Fake guru programs — exclusive masterminds with recycled generic advice
